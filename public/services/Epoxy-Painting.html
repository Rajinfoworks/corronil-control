Epoxy Painting: Complete Service Overview

Epoxy painting is a protective coating technique widely used in industrial environments for its durability, chemical resistance, and ability to withstand harsh conditions.

Our Process

  1. Surface Preparation: Cleaning and abrasive blasting for proper adhesion.
  2. Primer Application: Epoxy primer ensures strong bonding and corrosion resistance.
  3. Epoxy Coating: Multiple coats applied for thickness, durability, and chemical resistance.
  4. Curing: Proper drying and curing under controlled conditions.
  5. Final Inspection: Testing for thickness, adhesion, gloss, and durability.
