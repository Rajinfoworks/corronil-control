Zinc Spray Galvanizing & Metallizing

Advanced Corrosion Protection

CORRONiL CONTROL provides advanced Zinc Spray Galvanizing and Metallizing solutions for industrial structures, ensuring long-term durability against corrosion, heat, and wear.

Service Overview

Zinc Spray Galvanizing and Metallizing involve spraying molten zinc, aluminum, or stainless steel onto surfaces to provide a durable, corrosion-resistant coating for steel and iron structures.

Our Process

  1. Surface Preparation: Abrasive blasting for strong coating adhesion.
  2. Thermal Spray Coating: Molten zinc/aluminum applied via flame or arc spray.
  3. Layering: Multiple passes ensure uniform protection.
  4. Sealing: Optional sealers improve corrosion resistance.
  5. Inspection: Coating tested for thickness and durability.
