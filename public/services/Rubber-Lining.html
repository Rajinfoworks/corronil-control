Rubber Lining: Complete Service Overview

Rubber lining is a durable protective solution for industrial surfaces, preventing wear, chemical corrosion, and mechanical damage in pipelines, tanks, and equipment.

Our Process

  1. Surface Preparation: Cleaning and abrasive blasting for a clean, roughened surface.
  2. Adhesive Application: Specialized bonding agents applied to ensure proper adhesion.
  3. Rubber Lining Installation: Sheets or liquid rubber applied and pressed onto surfaces.
  4. Curing: Controlled temperature and time to achieve a solid, durable lining.
  5. Inspection & Testing: Checking thickness, adhesion, and defect-free coverage.
