Sand Blasting: Complete Service Overview

Sand blasting is an essential process that cleans and prepares surfaces for coatings, painting, or metallizing. It ensures strong adhesion, extended lifespan, and corrosion protection.

Our Process

  1. Surface Preparation: Rust, old paint, grease, and contaminants are removed using high-pressure blasting.
  2. Abrasive Blasting: Controlled abrasive material ensures thorough cleaning and surface profiling.
  3. Cleaning & Inspection: Blasted surface is inspected for uniformity and proper adhesion profile.
  4. Post-Blasting Protection: Immediate coating/primer application prevents flash rusting.
