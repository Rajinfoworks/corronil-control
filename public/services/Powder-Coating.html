Powder Coating: Complete Service Overview

Powder coating is a sustainable finishing technique that ensures durability, chemical resistance, and a superior aesthetic for industrial surfaces.

Our Process

  1. Surface Preparation: Sandblasting, cleaning, or chemical treatment for a clean base.
  2. Pre-treatment: Conversion coating applied for better adhesion and corrosion resistance.
  3. Powder Application: Electrostatic spray ensures uniform thickness and coverage.
  4. Curing: High-temperature oven baking for strong bonding and durability.
  5. Inspection: Final checks for coating thickness, gloss, adhesion, and durability.
